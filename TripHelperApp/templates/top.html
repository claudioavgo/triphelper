#1 France


France: 48.4 million visitors


France encompasses medieval citles, alpine villages, and Mediterranean beaches. Whenever you visit France, don't forget to visit the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Palace of Versailles.

#2 Mexico


Mexico: 31.9 million visitors


Visitors are attracted to Mexico's beach destinations and its cultural tourism. Chichén Itzá, Tulum and Museo Nacional de Antropología are some must-visit tourist destinations in Mexico.

#3 Spain


Spain: 31.2 million visitors


Spain has a diverse geography and culture. It is home to the infamous Royal Palace of Madrid, Prado Museum, and La Sagrada Familia.

#4 Turkey


Turkey: 29.9 million visitors


Located at the juncture of Southeast Europe and West Asia, Turkey has beautiful tourist sites like the Hagia Sophia, The Blue Mosque and the Topkapi Palace Museum.

#5 Italy


Italy: 26.9 million visitors


Italy offers delicious food and is home to tourist destinations of historical importance and serene views. The Colosseum, Pantheon and Trevi Fountain are some of the most- visited places in Italy.
